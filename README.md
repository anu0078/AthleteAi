{Your Free AI Coach That Actually Gets It}

Hey there! 👋 

Ever dreamed of having a personal coach but couldn't afford one? Yeah, we've been there. That's exactly why we built AthleteAI.

This isn't just another fitness app. It's built specifically for Indian athletes who have the talent and drive but not the money for expensive coaching. Upload a quick photo, tell us your sport, and boom - you get a personalized 4-week training plan that uses stuff you already have at home. Plus proper Indian meal plans that won't break the bank.

Works in Hindi too, because why should language be a barrier to your dreams?

Because every athlete deserves a shot at greatness, regardless of their wallet size.

What Makes This Special?

1== Smart Workouts That Make Sense
No fancy gym needed. We'll show you how to get stronger using water bottles, chairs, and that old rope in your garage. Each plan is tailored to your sport - whether you're into kushti or cricket.

2== Real Indian Food Plans
Forget expensive protein powders. We'll tell you exactly what to eat (dal, roti, sabzi - the good stuff) and how much, all under ₹150 a day. Your mom will approve.

3== Track Your Journey
Log your workouts, see your streak grow, and watch yourself get better day by day. It's pretty satisfying, honestly.

4== Photo Analysis That Actually Helps
Upload a pic and our AI will spot things you might miss - posture issues, muscle imbalances, stuff like that. Then it suggests specific exercises to fix them.

5== Speaks Your Language
English or Hindi - whatever feels comfortable. We're not here to make things harder for you.

6== Sports We Cover

Wrestling (Kushti), Kabaddi, Athletics, Boxing (Mukkebazi), Football, Cricket, Volleyball, Badminbadminton, Hockey, Kho-Kho, Weightlifting, Archery, Judo, Swimming

Don't see your sport? Drop us a message - we're always adding more!
